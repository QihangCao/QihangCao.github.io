About Me

I am Qihang Cao, a Ph.D. student at Nanyang Technological University (NTU) in Singapore. Before that, I received my master's degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), advised by Prof. Genke Yang.

I am currently advised by Prof. Jiawang Bian. My research interests lie in spatial intelligence and 3D LLMs, with a focus on 3D scene understanding, spatial reasoning, and multimodal systems.
